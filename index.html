About Us

Vision

Established in June 2017, Ho Node provides an inclusive space for digital skill training in emerging technologies, co-working space, business incubation and acceleration program, inclusive community to support innovation, and digital outreaches to promote STEM in basic schools in Ghana. We are in Hse. 26 Wudake Street, five minutes walk from Ho Technical University.

Our Believe

Talent and Innovations are everywhere. However, opportunities are not evenly distributed. Ho Node exists to bring global and national opportunities, entrepreneurship and digital skills acceleration programs into our community, Ho, Volta Region of Ghana
